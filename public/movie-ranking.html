Movies Watched in 2025

Top 3 Movies for January:

The Darkest Minds

Brings back nostalgia from my teenage years. It portrays beautiful connections with the characters and how their differences can be used to create a powerful entity.

Van Heilsing

Old sci-fi movie that has a great take on vampires and werewolves. It has great characters and some dark humour sprinkled here and there.

Red One

A different take on Christmas. It is a movie about how an evil witch tried to steal Santa Claus and Dwayne Johnson along with Captain America try to get him back.
